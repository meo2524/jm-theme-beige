### 🚀 **Debutify 8.13 — More Flexibility, Better Conversions & Smarter Store Experience**

Debutify **8.13** is here! 🎉  
This release introduces **new sections, powerful storefront widgets, enhanced subscription layouts, and multiple UX improvements,** helping merchants build **more engaging, visually consistent, and conversion-focused storefronts.**

From the new **Fly to Cart widget** to **advanced subscription layouts and improved bundle structure,** Debutify 8.13 enhances **store performance, customization flexibility, and customer experience** - while keeping everything simple to manage.

## 🛠️ Improvements & Enhancements

### 🎨 **Title**

- **Title block** and **theme settings** now include **title color controls** for better branding consistency.

### 🖼️ **Image Consistency**

- Improved **image handling across the entire theme** for better visual consistency.

### 💬 **Testimonials Section**

- **Border styling improved** to keep the section layout cleaner and visually consistent.

### ➖ **Separator**

- **Color scheme support introduced** in the separator section for greater styling flexibility.

### 📦 **Cart Upsell Bundle**

- Optimized **HTML structure** to reduce unnecessary markup and improve performance.

### 📌 **Custom Column**

- Introduced a **sticky content option** allowing blocks to stay visible while scrolling.

### 🖼️ **Image Slider Block**

- Introduced new customization options including **description support, badge alignment, and badge text controls** for greater design flexibility.

### 🔖 **Product Icon With Text**

- Introduced **alignment control, color scheme support, and separate card layout options** to improve styling and layout flexibility.

### 🛒 **Product as Variant**

- Improved **product as variant** for **smoother selection and better user experience.**

### 📢 **Horizontal Ticker**

- **Border radius options introduced** with global and default settings for better styling control.

### 🔄 **Product Subscription**

- Expanded subscription customization with **vertical and minimal layouts, image and radio support, flexible selection styles, and better frequency display options.**

### 🖼️ **Custom Column Icon With Image**

- Introduced **color scheme options** for improved design flexibility.

### 🛡 **Guarantee Section**

- **Border styling improved** for a cleaner look and better consistency with the overall theme design.

### 📊 **Main Product & Featured Product**

- **Description block support introduced** for both **Featured Product** and **Main Product** sections for greater layout flexibility and content control.

### 📑 **Content Tabs**

- Improved the **scrolling bar behavior** for a smoother and more responsive browsing experience.

### 🎞 **Image Video Slider**

- Introduced a **background overlay option for images** to enhance visual focus and content readability.

### 🏷️ **Featured On**

- Introduced a **custom logo spacing** option for **desktop** to allow **better control over logo layout** and **alignment.**

### 📦 **Quantity Breaks**

- **Quantity Breaks** now include new **trust-building add-ons** to help **increase customer confidence** and **support higher conversions.**

    - **Money Guarantee**
    - **Shipping Protection**

### 🧹 **Code Optimization**

- Removed **unused HTML** from **Article Card, Collection Card,** and **Product Card** for a **cleaner structure** and **better performance**.

- Removed **duplicate Organization schema** that was loading twice.

### 📰 **Featured Blog**

- **Enhanced the Featured Blog section** with improved **article tag** visibility and a **content background overlay** option.

### 🎛️ **Header Menu**

- Improved **header menu interaction** to ensure proper **click behavior** when **the hover dropdown** option is **enabled**.

## 🐞 **Bug Fixes & Stability Updates**
